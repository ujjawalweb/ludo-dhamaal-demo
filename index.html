GameVariants

4 House
4 House is a common variation of gameplay with no condition related to physical Ludo. The Player has to join the Ludo board by a tap on the play now button, and will be assigned with 4 pawns in the base of the Ludo board. The game will start when all opponents join the game table. The player must try to open the pawn from the base and take it to the house following the board path. The player has to keep the pawn safe from the opponent to reach the house.

Terms & Conditions
  • All 4 pawns should be in the house to win the game, and the player should be the first to do so.
  • Players can open the pawn at dice value 6 only.
  • Each player will get 15 sec to roll the dice and move the pawn.
1 House
1 House is a Ludo game play variation in Ludo Dhamaal application. Player have to join the Ludo board by tap on play now button, will be assigned with 4 pawns in the base of Ludo board. Game will start when all opponents join the game table. Player must try to open the pawn from base and take it to the house following the board path. Player have to keep pawn safe from opponent to reach house.

Terms & Condition
  • Player have to house 1 pawn from 4 to win the game, player should be the first to do so.
  • Player can open the pawn at dice value 6 only.
  • Each player will get 15 sec to roll the dice and move pawn.
Timer
Timer is a new variation of game, which shows the ludo skills of player in pre-defined time. In this format, player will be assigned with 4 open pawns. Player just need to move the pawn from start position to house following the path of ludo board. A timer will be running on right side which moves towards finish. All players will get the same number of turns to score the maximum points. Points will show on the base of board.

Terms & Condition
  • All pawns will be on start position or open.
  • Game will finish as soon as timer countdown comes to 00:00 and each player get 15 sec to roll the dice and move the pawn.
  • All players will get same number of turns.
  • Player have to score maximum points to win the game.
  • Each step move reward 1 point and making pawn house will reward 56 points.
  • The player who will have maximum number of points after the timer comes to 00:00 will win the game.
Challenge
Challenge in Ludo Dhamaal helps you to join the board on which opponent already available. All open tables which are ready to play will show in your challenge screen. Player can also create a challenge for others, just need to click on play now button. Player will be on board and will wait for opponent, this action will be challenge for other players.

Terms & Condition
  • Challenge game can be Timer, 1 House or 4 House.
  • Player can create and accept the challenge.

How To Play

* All players will be assigned 4 pawns in the base of the Ludo Dhamaal board. .
* The game will start when a maximum number of players join the board.
* In order to take the pawn out of the base, the player needs to get number 6 on the dice.
* The player will be eligible to move the pawn from the start position, once the pawn is out of the base.
* The goal of each pawn is to move the pawn by following the path of the board and reach the house to make that pawn win.
* The player must reach all four pawns to the house by moving through the path of the board.
* The player must keep their pawn safe/uncut from the opponent pawn, if the opponent pawn comes on the tile of your pawn,    you will be in the base and need to start again.
* The objective of Ludo is to bring all pawns to the respective house with ensuring that you are the first to do so.
* If all your pawns will be in the house, you will be declared as winner of the game.
